What is neuroplasicity and what are the benefits of understanding it?

According to the definition on Google when you look up neuroplasticity, it is, the ability of the brain to form and reorganize synaptic connections, especially in response to learning or experience or following injury.

Understanding neuroplasticity helps benefit people in their ability to learn new things, enhance existing cognitive abilities and learnings, and can also strengthen cognitive areas where function has been lost or has declined over time. In some other cases, neuroplasticity can help stroke and traumatic brain injury patients recover and improve their cognitive function.

How can you improve neuroplasicity?

Some key ways to increase neuroplasticity is simply continuous practice in what you are learning and what you are trying to achieve. By putting in work and practising something their neural networks shape themselves according to that activity or practice. By further shaping those neural networks, you are increasing neuroplasticity.

Growth Mindset


What is a growth mindset and why is it relevant?

Growth mindset is a mindset in which you believe that you can always continue to grow your intelligence, that your intelligence level is no set, or predefined, when you are born and that it is something you can grow and expand on by putting in the effort and continuing to learn and push yourself. It is a way of viewing challenges and setbacks. People with a growth mindset believe that even though they may struggle or not understand something initially, with enough work and effort, their skills and understanding will improve over time.
